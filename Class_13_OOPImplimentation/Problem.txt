1.       Create C# console application to take and print the following employee information:
                i.     ID
              ii.     Name
             iii.     Designation
            iv.     Date of Birth
              v.     Joining Date
            vi.     Role Plays
 
The application will follow the following instructions strictly:
a.	Principle of OOP must be used to develop the application.
b.	Role Plays must be implemented. Roles are unlimited
c.	Employee must inherit person. Person cannot be instantiated. Employee cannot be inherited.
d.	Enum will be used for designations.
